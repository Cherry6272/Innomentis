About Us

Innomentis is an experiential learning organization committed to making robotics, coding, and STEM education simple, engaging, and fun for young learners. Through hands-on activities, innovative projects, and real-world problem solving, we inspire children to explore technology with creativity and confidence. Our programs are designed to build strong foundational skills, spark curiosity, and empower every child to become confident learners in a rapidly evolving digital world.

Vision

To build a smarter, self-reliant India by nurturing young minds through hands-on, experiential learning that inspires innovation, creativity, and purposeful problem-solving.

Mission

Innomentis aims to transform how students learn and think by bringing hands-on STEM-based experiences into everyday education. We strive to cultivate innovation, problem-solving, and creative thinking by merging play with purpose. Our child-centric approach helps every learner feel confident in technology, creating a future where nurturing a generation ready to shape India's digital economy is both impactful and joyful.

Objectives

Our objective is to ignite curiosity and inspire innovation in young minds by delivering hands-on robotics and technology learning through interactive offline workshops in schools and engaging online classes. We strive to create a complete learning journey that culminates in our annual RTC Fest — a celebration where students showcase their creativity, skills, and passion for future-ready STEM fields.
