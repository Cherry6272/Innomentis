Our Programmes

Robotics Tech Course – Schools

This 3-year Robotics Course introduces students to the foundational concepts of engineering, design, and technology. Through hands-on building and programming activities, they gradually learn how robotics systems work and apply problem-solving in real-world contexts. The course nurtures curiosity and builds strong technical confidence, preparing young learners for the future of innovation.

Beyond the Classroom

From RTC Fest showcases to community hackathons, our learners get continuous opportunities to collaborate, present, and celebrate their creations. Mentorship, design thinking challenges, and industry interactions ensure every child experiences the full excitement of building with technology.

Robotics Tech Course – Online

Delivered one-on-one online, this course lets students learn at their own pace with personalized attention. Guided virtual lessons and interactive programming exercises keep learners engaged while building confidence, creativity, and strong foundational engineering skills—all from home.

AIML For Advanced

Our AIML course equips students with strong foundations in artificial intelligence and machine learning. Through practical coding, model training, and data-driven projects, learners understand how intelligent systems work and how to apply them to real scenarios, building the confidence to excel in AI-powered careers.

Building an AI & ML Learning Ecosystem

This advanced ecosystem combines practical coding, model training, and hands-on data projects that teach students how to prototype intelligent solutions. Learners practice analytical thinking and innovation, gaining the readiness to thrive in an ever-evolving AI landscape.

What Students Learn

Innoventis Learning Pillars

Each programme is anchored to five core pillars that empower future innovators—basics of robotics, coding fundamentals, creative design, logical problem-solving, and engineering concepts. These pillars ensure every learner receives a holistic STEM foundation while nurturing imagination and confidence.
